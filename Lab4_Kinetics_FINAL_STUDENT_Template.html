Background Summary

The overall reaction is:

CH₃COCH₃ + Br₂ → CH₃COCH₂Br + HBr

The rate law has the form: rate = k [acetone]ⁿ [H⁺]ᵐ [Br₂]ᵖ

Your goal is to determine the exponents n, m, and p, and calculate the rate constant k.

Key concept: Br₂ is only in the fast step of the mechanism. The rate-determining step is the acid-catalyzed enolization, which does not involve Br₂. Therefore p = 0 (zero order in Br₂), and your plot of [Br₂] vs time should be a straight line.
