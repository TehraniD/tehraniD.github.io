  • Prioritize Module 7 - It has the most questions (22) and includes Renal Disorders (8 questions alone!)
  • Master the "Big 3" in Module 6 - Diabetes Insipidus, Endocrine Disorders, and Neurological Disorders (3 questions each)
  • Don't neglect Module 4 - Osteoarthritis and Osteoporosis are heavily tested
  • Know your terminology - Multiple modules test system-specific terms
  • Physical exam findings matter - 3 questions in Module 5 focus on this
  • All multiple choice format - No complex question types means you can focus on content mastery
  • Use the Jeopardy game - Practice with the Week 6 Jeopardy game to reinforce Module 6 concepts
  • Review lecture notes and practice quizzes - Your Week 6 materials are excellent review resources
