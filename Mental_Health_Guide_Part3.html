Part 3: Substance Abuse Disorders

Part 1: Bipolar & Mood Part 2: Thought Disorders Part 3: Substance Use

Concept Overview

  • Assessment & Screening: Screening tools (e.g., CAGE, CIWA) and identifying physical risk factors during intoxication and withdrawal.
  • Alcohol Use Disorder: Managing withdrawal, delirium tremens, and utilizing medications (Disulfiram, Chlordiazepoxide).
  • Opioid Use Disorder: Distinguishing overdose from withdrawal, and the roles of Naloxone (reversal), Methadone, and Buprenorphine (maintenance).
