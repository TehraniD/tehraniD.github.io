The nurse is conducting a mental status exam. The nurse should understand that which of the following is included in a mental status exam? (Select all that apply)

Select ALL that apply, then click Submit

Correct Answer Rationale: In a mental status exam, the nurse should assess thought content, behavior, and eye contact. Thought content evaluates the client's cognitive patterns, behavior refers to the client's physical actions, and eye contact is an important component of interaction assessment. Vital signs and nutritional intake are not part of the mental status exam.

Correct Answer Rationale: Orientation refers to the client's awareness of time, place, and person. Judgment is evaluated to determine the client's ability to make reasoned decisions. Insight refers to the client's awareness and understanding of their condition. Blood pressure and respiratory rate are vital signs that are not part of the mental status exam.
