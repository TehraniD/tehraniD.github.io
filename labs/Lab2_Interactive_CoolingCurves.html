�️ What's Happening on the Road?

Rock salt (NaCl) breaks into Na⁺ and Cl⁻ ions on the road. These particles block water molecules from forming ice crystals, requiring a lower temperature to freeze. That's why roads stay clear!

💰 Road Salt Economics

Rock salt costs ~$60/ton but only works above -9°C. CaCl₂ costs ~$300/ton but works down to -29°C. Cities switch to the expensive stuff during polar vortex events!
