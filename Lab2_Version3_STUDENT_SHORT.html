Key Formula

kf(water) = 1.86 °C/m | NaCl: i=2 | CaCl₂: i=3

Procedure

Part A: Calibration (5 min)

  1. Place thermometer in ice water bath for 2 minutes
  2. Record reading: °C (should be 0°C)
  3. Correction factor = 0 − reading = °C

Part B: Prepare Solutions (10 min)

  1. Label 4 beakers: Control, Light, Heavy, Premium
  2. Add 50 g crushed ice to each beaker
  3. Add salt as shown in table above
  4. Stir each solution for 30 seconds

Part C: Measure Temperatures (20 min)

  1. Insert thermometer into each solution
  2. Wait until temperature stabilizes (~2 min per solution)
  3. Record minimum temperature reached
  4. Repeat for all 4 solutions
