How to Use This Tool

A quick guide for staff. Open this any time by clicking the ? button.

Viewing the schedule

The schedule loads automatically. Each week shows in-person classes prominently and online classes in a clearly separated section below.

Editing for a new term

  1. Click Edit Mode in the top bar.
  2. Update Term Information (name, dates, weeks).
  3. Add, edit, or remove rows in the course tables.
  4. Click Save & Render.

Saving and reusing templates

  1. Click Export JSON to download a template file.
  2. Next term, click Import and choose that JSON to restore everything.
  3. Then make small edits (dates, instructors, etc.) and re-export.

Sharing with your department

  • Upload this HTML file to a SharePoint document library.
  • Or attach it to a Teams channel as a tab (Website tab).
  • Or share via OneDrive — anyone with the link can view and download.

Printing / saving as PDF

Click Print / PDF. The edit panel and toolbar are hidden in print, giving you a clean printable schedule.
